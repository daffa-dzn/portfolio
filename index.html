E-commerce Analysis and Seller Churn Prediction Model

Seller retention is crucial for the sustenance of revenue for e-commerce platforms like Olist. This project explores transactional and operational data to identify high-performing sellers, buyer patterns, and predict seller churn. With Python (pandas, seaborn, matplotlib, XGBoost) and Tableau, my team developed insights and a prediction model that achieves a 0.92 recall rate for high-performing sellers. The model delivers 5.9x cost savings, reducing retention expenses from R$36,761 to R$6,204 compared to traditional approaches.
